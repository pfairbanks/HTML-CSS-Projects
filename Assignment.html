Happy Go Lucky Dog Training explains the importance of properly training your dog :

Managing and training your dog can help prevent inappropriate play which can escalate into aggression. Puppies first learn to play with their littermates. Their play consists of a lot of rough-and-tumble activity and tons of play biting. A dog playing in this fashion with an adult or child can be potentially dangerous. It is our responsibility to teach our dogs appropriate games they which can play with us.
